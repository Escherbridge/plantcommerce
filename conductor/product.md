# Aevani — Product Guide

## Vision
Make sustainable agriculture accessible to everyone by providing high-quality products, comprehensive educational resources, and innovative affiliate marketing tools that grow the sustainable farming community.

## Target Users

### Primary Segments
1. **Hobbyist Gardeners** — Individuals interested in sustainable growing at home (hydroponics, aquaponics, agroforestry)
2. **Commercial Farmers** — Professional farmers seeking sustainable agriculture supplies and techniques
3. **Educators & Institutions** — Schools, universities, and research institutions teaching sustainable farming
4. **Affiliate Marketers & Content Creators** — Influencers and bloggers monetizing sustainable agriculture recommendations

## Problems Solved

1. **Access to Products** — Difficult to find quality sustainable agriculture products (hydroponics, aquaponics, silvopasture, agroforestry) consolidated in one trusted marketplace
2. **Monetization for Creators** — Content creators in the sustainability space lack tools to earn from product recommendations
3. **Affiliate Synergy** — The platform creates a flywheel: affiliates drive traffic, earn commissions, and produce educational content that attracts more customers

## Key Features

### E-commerce Core
- Multi-category product catalog (Hydroponics, Aquaponics, Silvopasture, Agroforestry)
- Shopping cart with discount codes and affiliate attribution
- Secure checkout with order tracking
- Role-based user accounts (Customer, Affiliate, Admin)

### Affiliate Marketing System
- Automated affiliate link generation with unique tracking codes
- Real-time click tracking, conversion monitoring, and earnings dashboard
- Configurable commission rates with automatic calculations
- 30-day cookie-based attribution

### Content Management System
- Educational content: guides, blog posts, FAQs
- Rich text editor with SEO optimization
- Media management via Google Cloud Storage
- Content categorization and search

### Admin Backend
- Product, order, user, and content CRUD
- Analytics dashboard for business intelligence
- Audit logging

## Success Metrics

| Category | KPIs |
|----------|------|
| **Revenue & Orders** | Monthly revenue, average order value, repeat purchase rate |
| **Affiliate Growth** | Number of active affiliates, affiliate-driven revenue, affiliate conversion rates |
| **User Engagement** | Active users, content page views, time on site, newsletter signups |
