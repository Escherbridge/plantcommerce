# Aevani: Project Synthesis

## 1. Project Vision & Mission

Aevani is a full-stack e-commerce platform designed to promote and sell products related to sustainable agriculture, including hydroponics, aquaponics, silvopasture, and agroforestry. The core mission is to make sustainable agriculture accessible to everyone by providing high-quality products, comprehensive educational resources, and an innovative affiliate marketing system to support and grow the sustainable farming community.

## 2. Strategic Pivot: From E-commerce to Media Platform

A deep analysis of the business model reveals a critical insight: competing with established e-commerce giants on price and logistics is a losing battle. The project's true potential lies not in direct retail, but in becoming a **community-driven media and affiliate platform.**

**The Verdict:** The project must pivot from a capital-intensive e-commerce model to a lean, scalable affiliate and content platform. The goal is to build an audience first, then monetize that audience through affiliate commissions. You are not building an e-commerce store; you are building a media company that facilitates sales.

**Key Advantages of the Pivot:**
*   **Lower Capital:** Requires 10x less capital by eliminating the need for inventory.
*   **Higher Margins:** Pure commission margins (e.g., 5%) are more profitable than gross retail margins (e.g., 30%) burdened by operational costs.
*   **Scalability:** Content and community scale exponentially, while retail scales linearly.
*   **Higher Valuation:** Media and platform businesses command significantly higher valuation multiples than retail.

## 3. The Business Model: Affiliate-Driven Community

The core of the new model is to create a "flywheel" effect:

1.  **Create High-Quality Content:** Develop expert guides, reviews, and tutorials on sustainable agriculture. This builds trust and attracts an audience through SEO.
2.  **Build an Engaged Community:** Nurture the audience through newsletters, forums (Discord/Slack), and social media.
3.  **Recruit Passionate Affiliates:** Empower community members and content creators to earn commissions by promoting products they believe in.
4.  **Partner with Brands:** Leverage the audience and affiliate network to negotiate favorable commission rates (10-15%) with sustainable agriculture brands.

**Revenue Streams:**
*   **Primary:** Commission on sales generated by affiliates (targeting a 3-5% net take-rate).
*   **Secondary:** Premium memberships for affiliates (offering advanced tools and higher commissions), and sponsored placements for brand partners.

This model positions Aevani as the definitive "Impact Marketplace" for sustainable agriculture, a niche where values-alignment and community are a powerful defense against giants like Amazon.

## 4. Current Project Status

The platform's technical foundation is incredibly strong and well-suited for this pivot.

**Build Status: Production Ready**
*   **Modern Tech Stack:** SvelteKit, tRPC, PostgreSQL, and Lucia Auth provide a scalable, secure, and type-safe foundation.
*   **Feature Complete (for MVP):** All core routes for e-commerce, affiliate marketing, content management, and admin functions are built and integrated with the backend.
*   **Authentication:** Robust, role-based authentication (`Customer`, `Affiliate`, `Admin`) is fully implemented.
*   **Component Library:** A consistent UI is established with DaisyUI and a Storybook component library.

**Critical Gaps to Address:**
While the application is technically sound, it lacks the infrastructure for audience growth:
*   **SEO:** Missing fundamental SEO components like sitemaps, structured data, and Open Graph tags.
*   **Analytics:** No analytics or conversion tracking is in place to measure performance.
*   **Content Infrastructure:** The CMS is functional but needs a rich text editor and image optimization to be effective.

## 5. The Path Forward: A 90-Day Action Plan

The immediate priority is to shift focus from building platform features to **building an audience.**

### Days 1-30: Foundation & Validation ($5K Budget)
*   **Goal:** Acquire the first 500 email subscribers and validate content strategy.
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Fix Critical Gaps:** Implement SEO and analytics infrastructure.
    2.  **Publish Cornerstone Content:** Create 5 high-quality, in-depth guides on core topics (e.g., "Beginner's Guide to Hydroponics").
    3.  **Launch Newsletter:** Set up an email list and a simple lead magnet.
    4.  **Test Keywords:** Run a small Google Ads budget to identify high-intent keywords.

### Days 31-60: Build Traction ($10K Budget)
*   **Goal:** Grow to 2,500 email subscribers and 10,000 monthly visitors.
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Accelerate Content:** Publish 3 articles per week and start a YouTube channel.
    2.  **Build Community:** Launch a Discord or Facebook group to engage the audience.
    3.  **Generate Backlinks:** Guest post on relevant blogs to build domain authority.

### Days 61-90: Monetization Test ($15K Budget)
*   **Goal:** Achieve the first $5,000 in affiliate revenue.
*   **Actions:**
    1.  **Launch Affiliate Program:** Onboard the first 10-20 micro-influencer affiliates.
    2.  **Create "Recommendation" Content:** Publish product reviews and comparisons with affiliate links (starting with Amazon Associates as a baseline).
    3.  **Optimize for Conversion:** A/B test landing pages and calls-to-action.

**Success at 90 days ($5K in revenue and 25K monthly visitors) is the green light to scale investment and go full-time.**

## 6. Conclusion: A Viable, High-Potential Opportunity

Aevani is a well-conceived project with a strong technical foundation and a noble mission. However, success is contingent on a strategic pivot to a **content-first, affiliate-driven model.**

The path forward is not about building more features, but about building an audience. By focusing relentlessly on creating value through content and community for the next 6-12 months, Aevani can establish itself as the leading voice in the sustainable agriculture space and build a highly scalable, profitable, and impactful business.
