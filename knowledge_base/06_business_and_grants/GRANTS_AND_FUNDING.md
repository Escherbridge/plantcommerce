# Grants, Funding & Government Procurement

## Federal Grant Programs

### SARE (Sustainable Agriculture Research and Education)
- **Website**: sare.org/grants/
- **Funding**: Varies by region; hundreds of thousands per project
- **Cycle**: Pre-proposals ~November, Full proposals ~February, Awards ~August
- **Focus**: On-farm research, education, farmer/rancher grants
- **Regions**: Northeast, North Central, Southern, Western (each has own office)
- **Tip**: Start with farmer/rancher grants (smaller, less competitive) to build track record

### RFSI (Resilient Food Systems Infrastructure)
- **Website**: ams.usda.gov/services/grants/rfsi
- **Total**: ~$400M distributed to states
- **Administered by**: State departments of agriculture (e.g., ISDA in Idaho)
- **Focus**: Aggregation, processing, manufacturing, storage, transport, distribution
- **Application**: Check state-level RFA status at ams.usda.gov/services/grants/rfsi/rfsi-rfa-status

### SCBG (Specialty Crop Block Grant Program)
- **Website**: ams.usda.gov/services/grants/scbgp
- **2025 Funding**: $72.9 million
- **Focus**: Non-commodity specialty crops market competitiveness
- **Deadline**: Typically July via Grants.gov
- **Contact**: SCBlockGrants@usda.gov
- **Use for**: Marketing campaigns, packaging design, agronomic development for aquaponic herbs/greens

### CIG (Conservation Innovation Grants)
- **Website**: nrcs.usda.gov/programs-initiatives/conservation-innovation-grants
- **Match**: 50% non-federal match required
- **2025**: $700M USDA commitment to regenerative agriculture (EQIP $400M + CSP $300M)
- **Guide**: nrcs.usda.gov/sites/default/files/2024-10/nrcs-cig-grant-funding-guide-october-2024.pdf
- **Focus**: Innovative conservation approaches

### OREI (Organic Agriculture Research and Extension Initiative)
- **Website**: nifa.usda.gov/grants/programs/organic-agriculture-research-extension-initiative-orei-program
- **Funding**: $44M/year
- **FY 2026**: Three separate NOFOs posted
- **Focus**: Whole farm planning, climate mitigation, resilience
- **Resource**: hub.ofrf.org/orei-and-org-repository

### CWDG (Community Wildfire Defense Grant Program)
- **Website**: fs.usda.gov/managing-land/fire/grants/cwdg
- **Round 3**: $200M+ available
- **Eligible**: Communities in WUI, at-risk areas
- **Primer**: firenetworks.org/community-wildfire-defense-grants-a-round-three-application-primer/
- **Key**: Requires Community Wildfire Protection Plan (CWPP)

## Federal Procurement Process

### Step 1: SAM.gov Registration
- Register at sam.gov
- Receive Unique Entity Identifier (UEI)
- Get CAGE code from Defense Logistics Agency
- REQUIRED for any federal bidding

### Step 2: SBA Certifications (explore eligibility)
- **8(a)**: Disadvantaged small businesses (9-year program)
- **HUBZone**: Historically underutilized business zones (sba.gov/federal-contracting/contracting-assistance-programs/hubzone-program)
- **WOSB**: Women-owned small businesses (5% federal goal)
- **SDVOSB**: Service-disabled veteran-owned
- Portal: certifications.sba.gov

### Step 3: NAICS Code Alignment
- **541620**: Environmental Consulting Services (primary for this work)
- **813312**: Environment, Conservation and Wildlife Organizations
- **237410**: Ecological Restoration (proposed new code for $9B industry)

### Step 4: Entry Strategy
- Start as SUBCONTRACTOR under established prime contractor
- Build "past performance" portfolio
- Graduate to prime contractor bidding
- Target: Idaho Dept of Lands, Idaho DEQ, BLM, Forest Service solicitations

## Carbon Credit Revenue

### Voluntary Market
- Average price: $6.34/credit
- High-quality ARR credits: up to $26/tCO2e
- Platforms: Indigo Ag, Regen Network, marketplace on your platform
- Standards: Verra VCS, Gold Standard

### Compliance Market
- EU ETS: ~$89/ton
- Growing in North America (California cap-and-trade)
- CORSIA (aviation offsets) creating new demand

### Key Requirements for Certification
1. Demonstrate additionality (wouldn't happen without carbon finance)
2. Independent third-party validation and verification
3. Ongoing monitoring (MRV: Measurement, Reporting, Verification)
4. Baseline soil carbon sampling before project starts
