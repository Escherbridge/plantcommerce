# Product Catalogue: Regenerative Agriculture Ecosystem

## Category 1: Controlled Environment Agriculture Systems
### 1.1 Aquaponic Systems
- **Decoupled Commercial System**: Separate aquaculture + hydroponic loops, optimized for fruiting crops (+36% yield), independent sterilization, FSMA compliant. Target: commercial growers.
- **Coupled Starter Kit**: Closed-loop beginner system, optimized for leafy greens (+31% vegetative biomass). Target: home/community growers.
- **Off-Grid Solar Aquaponics Kit**: 12V DC pumps, PV array, deep-cycle batteries, deep-sleep microcontroller (67% energy reduction). Target: remote/permaculture sites.

### 1.2 Hydroponic Systems
- **Deep Water Culture (DWC) Modules**: Stackable 4-foot raft beds with snap-together design
- **Nutrient Film Technique (NFT) Channels**: Custom extruded PVC/PP, chemically resistant
- **Vertical Growing Towers**: Blow-molded food-grade HDPE, modular stacking
- **Automated Dosing Controller**: ESP32-S2 based, Atlas Scientific EZO sensors, peristaltic pumps, cloud dashboard

### 1.3 Greenhouse & Climate Control
- **Passive Solar Greenhouse Kit**: Thermal mass design, no electrical climate control needed
- **IoT Environmental Control Unit**: Temperature, humidity, CO2 management tied to phenological stage
- **Microbubble/Nanobubble Aeration System**: Superior gas-liquid mass transfer for dissolved oxygen
